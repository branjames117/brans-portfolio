about

I'm Brandon James Hoskins, a full-stack web developer currently in development at Vanderbilt University Coding Boot Camp. My experiences include MERN stack, Next.js, and some Python, but I'm always eager to absorb new technologies. Outside of coding, my passions include medieval literature, creative writing, earth stewardship, wuxia films, Magic: The Gathering, and late night board game sessions with friends and family.

Though I did plenty of HTML, DHTML, and CSS as well as some C++ in my teens, my professional coding journey began in February of 2021 with an exploration into Python scripting, then developed through several Udemy courses to culminate in the Vanderbilt Boot Camp, to be followed by a lifelong learning quest to remain on the cutting edge.

Originally from Detroit, Michigan, I currently reside outside of Nashville, Tennessee, with my wife, daughter, a cat, a dog, and neighborhood squirrels.
