I'm Brandon James Hoskins, a full-stack web developer currently in development at Vanderbilt University Coding Boot Camp. My experiences include MERN stack, Next.js, and some Python, but I'm always eager to absorb new technologies. Originally from Detroit, Michigan, I currently reside outside of Nashville, Tennessee, with my wife, daughter, a cat, a dog, and neighborhood squirrels.
